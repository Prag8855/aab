This is how you create a Berlin service account (*Service-Konto Berlin*). You need a service account to [get a *Kitagutschein*](/guides/kitagutschein), or [get a *Gewerbeschein*](/guides/gewerbeschein).

## How to create an account

**[Create the account here](https://konto.service.berlin.de/skb/Account/Register)**. The account creation form is in German, but it's very simple. Just use the [English instructions](https://www.berlin.de/ea/en/application/login-service-account-berlin/help_for_registration.pdf).

When you create the account, select security level 1 (*Sicherheitsstufe 1*).

[![Berlin Service-Konto form, step 1](/images/service-konto-berlin-registration-1.png "Fill the form. Select \"Sicherheitsstufe 1\"")](/images/service-konto-berlin-registration-1.png)

[![Berlin Service-Konto form, step 2](/images/service-konto-berlin-registration-2.png "Tick the box, and click \"Registrierung abschliessen\"")](/images/service-konto-berlin-registration-2.png)

[![Berlin Service-Konto form, last step](/images/service-konto-berlin-registration-3.png)](/images/service-konto-berlin-registration-3.png)

[![Berlin Service-Konto activation email](/images/service-konto-berlin-registration-4.png "Wait for the account activation email. Click on the activation link.")](/images/service-konto-berlin-registration-4.png)

[![Berlin Service-Konto login form](/images/service-konto-berlin-registration-5.png "Log in")](/images/service-konto-berlin-registration-5.png)
