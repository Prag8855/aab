The VAT number (*Umsatzsteuernummer*, *Umsatzsteuer-Identifikationsnummer* or *USt-IdNr.*) is for businesses that charge [VAT](/glossary/Umsatzsteuer). In Germany, the VAT number has this format: **DE123456789**.

You can request a VAT number when you [register your business with the *Finanzamt*](/guides/fragebogen-zur-steuerlichen-erfassung). You can also [request a VAT number here](https://www.formulare-bfinv.de/ffw/action/invoke.do?id=ustid).

The VAT number is not the same as the tax number (*[[Steuernummer]]*).
