An *Altbau* or *Altbauwohnung* is an older apartment. *Altbau* apartments are often prized for their charm.

Compared to a *[[Neubau]]*, an *[[Altbau]]* has...

- Wood and brick structure
- Worse noise and temperature insulation
- Higher ceilings
- Hardwood floors
