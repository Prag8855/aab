Title: What is an Arbeitszeugnis
German_term: Arbeitszeugnis
English_term: Job reference letter

When you [leave a job](/guides/losing-your-job), you ask for a letter of reference (*Arbeitszeugnis*).

- A standard letter of reference explains when you worked for the employer, and what type of work you did. Your employer can't refuse to give you this letter.
- A qualified letter of reference (*qualifiziertes Arbeitszeugnis*) also includes an evaluation of your performance, and why you left the company.[^2] Your employer can refuse to give you this letter.
- A provisional letter of reference (*vorläufiges Arbeitszeugnis*) is a letter of reference you receive before your last day of work.[^3] Your employer can't refuse to give you this letter.

When you [leave a company](/guides/losing-your-job), your employer is required to give you an *Arbeitszeugnis*.[^1] Ask for it in your [resignation letter](/glossary/Kündigungsschreiben).
