A *Monatskarte* is a monthly pass.

Usually, this means a monthly *public transit* pass. For example, the [[BVG]] sells a *Monatskarte*. It lets you use public transportation for one month. In most cases, the [[Deutschland Ticket]] is better than a *Monatskarte*.
