There are 6 levels of German proficiency: [[A1]], [[A2]], [[B1]], B2, C1 and C2.

C1 German is fluent German. It means that you can...[^1]

- Understand TV shows and movies without too much effort.
- Understand specialised articles and longer technical instructions, even about unfamiliar topic.
- Speak fluently and spontaneously, without always searching for expressions.
- Write about complex subjects in a letter, an essay or a report.
