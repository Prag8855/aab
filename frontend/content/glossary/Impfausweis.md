---
Title: What is an Impfpass or Impfausweis?
German_term: Impfausweis
English_term: Vaccination pass
Description: A yellow booklet that contains your vaccination history.
Date_created: 2021-05-07
---

The vaccination pass (*Impfpass* or *Impfausweis*) is a [small yellow book](https://www.google.com/search?q=Impfausweis&tbm=isch) that shows which vaccines you have received. You can get one for free from your doctor, or from the *Gesundheitsamt*. You can also [buy it online](https://www.amazon.de/Impfpass-Impfbuch-Impfausweis-Internationale-Bescheinigung/dp/B08FX454T1?&linkCode=sl1&tag=nicolasboulia-21&linkId=9f94b653033684e8185aa5fcbf835d27&language=en_GB&ref_=as_li_ss_tl) or at any *Apotheke*.[^0]
