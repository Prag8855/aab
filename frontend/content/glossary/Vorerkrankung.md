Pre-existing health conditions are health problems that you had *before* getting [health insurance](/glossary/Krankenversicherung). If you have a pre-existing health condition, private health insurers might refuse to cover you, or charge higher prices.

Example pre-existing conditions:

- Psychotherapy
- Diabetes
- Cancer
- HIV/AIDS
- Addiction to alcohol or drugs
