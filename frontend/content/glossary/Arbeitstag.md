In Germany, business days (*Arbeitstage*) usually mean Monday to Friday, excluding [public holidays](/guides/public-holidays).[^0] This is different from working days (*[Werktage](/glossary/Werktag)*), which are usually Monday to Saturday, excluding public holidays.

[^0]: [Wikipedia](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arbeitstag#Rechtsfragen)
