A daycare voucher (*Kitagutschein*) allows you to get free childcare in Berlin. You need it to [send your child to a *Kita*](/guides/how-to-find-a-kita-in-berlin). The *Kitagutschein* says how much time your child can spend at the *[[Kita]]*.
