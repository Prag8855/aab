In Germany, the trade licence (*Gewerbeschein*) is a certificate that lets you to run your business in a city. This licence is issued by the trade office (*Gewerbeamt*) or by the *[[Ordnungsamt]]*. In Berlin, you can [apply for it online](https://www.berlin.de/ea/unsere-online-verfahren/service.758896.php/dienstleistung/121921/). Some businesses do not need a *Gewerbeschein*. For more details, see [this guide](/guides/freiberufler-or-gewerbe).
