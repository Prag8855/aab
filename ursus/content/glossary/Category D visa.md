A Category D visa is a long-term visa. It allows you to stay in Germany for more than 90 days. It's also called a [[National Visa]] or a [residence permit](/glossary/Aufenthaltstitel).

The [student visa](/glossary/Student%20visa), the [job seeker visa](/glossary/Job%20seeker%20visa), the [freelance visa](/glossary/Freelance%20visa), the [work visa](/glossary/Work%20Visa), the [[Blue Card]] and the [artist visa](/glossary/Artist%20visa) are types of Category D visas. The [tourist visa](/glossary/Schengen%20visa) is not a Category D visa.
