In Germany, the *Bildungsgutschein* is a voucher from the *[[Bundesagentur für Arbeit]]*. It pays for professional training for unemployed people. For example, you can use it to take programming classes or German classes.

The *Bildungsgutschein* is similar to the [AVGS voucher](/glossary/Aktivierungs-%20und%20Vermittlungsgutschein), but it's more for long-term training. It can cover months of training, and even a complete career change.[^0]
