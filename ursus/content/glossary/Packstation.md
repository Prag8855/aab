A *Packstation* is a place where you can receive packages. *Packstations* are sometimes more reliable than receiving packages at home. It works like a *[[Paketshop]]*, but fully automated.

![DHL Packstation](/images/DHL-Packstation.jpg "A DHL Packstation")
