The certificate of registration (*Meldebescheinigung*) is a *copy* of your registration certificate (*[[Anmeldebestätigung]]*). You can [request a *Meldebescheinigung* from the *Bürgeramt*](/guides/replacement-meldebescheinigung).

You need this document to confirm your address. For example, you often need it to apply for a [residence permit](/glossary/Aufenthaltstitel), [open a bank account](/guides/first-bank-account-in-germany), [get health insurance](/guides/german-health-insurance), or get a mobile phone contract.

For more details, see [this guide](/guides/replacement-meldebescheinigung).
