Title: What is a Schadenfreiheitsklasse?
German_term: Schadenfreiheitsklasse
English_term: Insurance class for drivers
Description: 
Date_created: 2020-08-10
Date_updated: 2022-04-20
Related_guides: guides/car-insurance-germany.md
---

German insurance companies give every driver class (*Schadenfreiheitsklasse*). If you don't make any insurance claims, your *Schadenfreiheitsklasse* gets higher every year. A high *Schadenfreiheitsklasse* means that you are a safe driver. If you have a high *Schadenfreiheitsklasse*, you pay less for insurance.
