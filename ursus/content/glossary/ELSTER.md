ELSTER (for *ELektronische STeuerERklärung*) is the online platform to [register a business](/guides/fragebogen-zur-steuerlichen-erfassung), file tax returns, file [VAT](/glossary/Umsatzsteuer) returns, and other tax matters. It's like an online *[[Finanzamt]]*.

ELSTER is run by the German government.
