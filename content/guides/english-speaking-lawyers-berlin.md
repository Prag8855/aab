This list of English, French and Spanish-speaking attorneys will help you with any legal issues you might encounter while living in Berlin. If you are looking for an immigration lawyer, there are some in the list below.

## How much does a lawyer cost?

**Around 200€ per hour.**[^0] If you have [legal insurance](/guides/legal-insurance), it will pay for the lawyer.

## Alternatives to a lawyer

- **Legal insurance**  
    Your [legal insurance](/guides/legal-insurance) might have a legal hotline.
- **Business and immigration hotline**  
    Call [0900 1 5293 6611](tel:0900152936611) to talk to Alexander von Engelhardt, a business and immigration lawyer. It costs 2€ per minute.
