Every year in January, [BSR](/glossary/Berliner%20Stadtreinigung) collects old Christmas trees in Berlin. This is a free service. The Christmas tree pickup dates can be found on the [BSR website](https://www.bsr.de/frohe-umwelt-fur-unsere-weihnachten-23170.php).

## Find the collection dates

Every year in January, [BSR](/glossary/Berliner%20Stadtreinigung) collects old Christmas trees. The collection dates (*Abholtermine*) depend on your address.

**[Tree disposal calendar](https://www.bsr.de/frohe-umwelt-fur-unsere-weihnachten-23170.php#:~:text=wann%20wird%20mein%20weihnachtsbaum%20abgeholt%3F)** - Berliner Stadtreinigung

[Calendar for January 2024](https://www.berlin.de/special/1590109-208-weihnachtsbaeume-abholtermine-der-bsr-im.html) - Berlin.de

## Leave your tree on the sidewalk

Remove all the decorations from the tree, and bring it outside 1 or 2 days before the collection date.[^0] Leave the tree on the sidewalk, close to the street. It should not get in the way or block the sidewalk.

![Old Christmas trees on a Berlin sidewalk](/images/berlin-christmas-tree-sidewalk.jpg)

The trees are used to make biogas.[^1]
