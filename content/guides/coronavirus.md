## Latest restrictions

**The full list of restrictions is [on Berlin.de](https://www.berlin.de/corona/en/measures/)**.

### Changes on February 2

You don't need to wear a mask on public transit, only in hospitals and nursing homes.[^0]

## Travelling to Berlin

You no longer need a test or a proof of vaccination to travel to Germany. There are no travel restrictions related to coronavirus.
