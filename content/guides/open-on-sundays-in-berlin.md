In Germany, most businesses are closed on Sundays and on [public holidays](/guides/public-holidays). Even supermarkets are closed, so people who move to Germany quickly learn to do their grocery shopping in advance. Fortunately, some grocery stores and pharmacies in Berlin are opened on Sundays. There are also special Sundays where stores are allowed to open.

## What is open on Sundays in Berlin?

On Sundays, most shops in Berlin must close. Only a few shops can stay open. Those shops are usually inside train stations.

### Supermarkets

Those train station supermarkets are open on Sundays:[^0]

- Friedrichstraße: [Edeka](https://goo.gl/maps/ya9LorGVAwUbLiQn9)
- Gesundbrunnen: [Denn's Biomarkt](https://g.page/denns-s-bahnhof?share)
- Hauptbahnhof: [SPAR Express](https://goo.gl/maps/Yn2ewT3PS27GcLvu9) and [REWE City](https://goo.gl/maps/AFGc4tZ8hVSZFie29)
- Lichtenberg: [Edeka](https://goo.gl/maps/kRz1Ryj4MpESzeAo7)
- Ostbahnhof: [REWE](https://goo.gl/maps/YRN5AhniErckRtQc7) and [Penny](https://goo.gl/maps/X6KDyzZQKfkxBkRZA)
- Potsdamer Platz: Spar Express and [go asia](https://goo.gl/maps/UmKhL6oN58TjaK5s9)
- Südkreuz: [Edeka](https://goo.gl/maps/QT3kv7QwvvfdseQy7)
- Zoologischer Garten: [Ullrich](https://goo.gl/maps/qFxfHAstAYaKTsTb6)

These supermarkets are very busy on Sundays. There is often a queue outside the Edeka at Friedrichstraße.

Grocery delivery services are closed on Sundays.[^1]

### Restaurants, Spätis and flea markets

Most restaurants are open on Sundays. Food delivery services also work.

Most [*Spätis*](/glossary/Sp%C3%A4ti) are open on Sundays.

 [Many flea markets](https://www.berlin.de/special/shopping/flohmaerkte/) (*Flohmarkt*) only open on Sundays.

### Museums

Some museums and tourist attractions stay open on Sundays.

## Special Sunday shopping days

On certain dates during the year, shops can open on Sundays. These are called *Verkaufsoffene Sonntage*. The stores that decide to open usually announce it in advance in flyers and advertisements.

### Next Sunday shopping days in Berlin

You can find[ the official list of Sunday shopping days](https://www.berlin.de/special/shopping/sonntag/1784369-1742809-verkaufsoffene-sonntage-in-berlin.html) on Berlin.de

### Shops that participate to *Verkaufsoffene Sonntage*

The following shops habitually participate to special shopping Sundays in Berlin:

- Most shopping malls
- Furniture stores
- Electronics stores
- Some supermarkets
