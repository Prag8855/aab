The cold rent (*Kaltmiete*) is your rent without utilities (*[[Nebenkosten]]*).

> Cold rent (*Kaltmiete*) + utilities (*Nebenkosten*) = warm rent (*[[Warmmiete]]*)

The warm rent (*[[Warmmiete]]*) is what you actually pay to your landlord every month.
