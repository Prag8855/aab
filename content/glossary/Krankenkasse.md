A *Krankenkasse* is a [public health insurance](/glossary/gesetzliche%20Krankenversicherung) company.

*Krankenkassen* are non-profit funds. They are not run by the government. There are over 90 of them, but their price and coverage are almost the same. The biggest *Krankenkassen* are [[Techniker Krankenkasse]], AOK and [Barmer](/out/barmer).
