The German Trade Register (*Handelsregister*) is a register of German businesses. All businesses must be listed in the *Handelsregister*, except small businesses (*[[Kleinunternehmer]]*), freelancers (*[[Freiberufler]]*) and companies constituted under civil law (*GbR*).
