The *Bildungsgutschein* is a voucher from the *[Agentur für Arbeit](/glossary/Bundesagentur%20f%C3%BCr%20Arbeit)*. It lets unemployed people take language classes and professional training for free.

It's similar to the [AVGS voucher](/glossary/Aktivierungs-%20und%20Vermittlungsgutschein), but it's more for long-term training. It can cover months of training, and even a complete career change.[^0]

**[More information](https://www.arbeitsagentur.de/arbeitslos-arbeit-finden/aktivierungs-vermittlungsgutschein-avgs)** (in German) - *[[Bundesagentur für Arbeit]]*
