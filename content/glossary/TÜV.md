The *TÜV* (*Technischer Überwachungsverein*) is the authority that inspects vehicles, elevators and many other things in Germany. Sometimes, people use the term *TÜV* for the vehicle inspection (*[[Hauptuntersuchung]]*) itself.

Dekra and KÜS are two other German inspection authorities.
