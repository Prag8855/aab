The *Bürgertelefon* is a free phone hotline in Germany. The phone number is **[115](tel:+4930115)**. You can call this number to access government services without a computer. In Berlin, you can call 115 to [get a *Bürgeramt* appointment](/guides/berlin-burgeramt-appointment) or a [vehicle registration office](/glossary/Kfz-Zulassungsstelle) appointment.

**[More information](https://service.berlin.de/buergertelefon/)** (in German) - Berlin.de
