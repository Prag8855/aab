---
Title: What is the Berlin Bürgertelefon?
German_term: Bürgertelefon
English_term: Citizen hotline
Description: A hotline you can call to access citizen services and get a Bürgeramt appointment.
Date_created: 2018-07-31
Related_guides: guides/berlin-burgeramt-appointment.md
---

The *Bürgertelefon* lets you access government services by phone. Their phone number is **[115](tel:+4930115)**. You can call it to book an appointment at the *[[Bürgeramt]]* and the [vehicle registration office](/glossary/Kfz-Zulassungsstelle).

**[More information](https://service.berlin.de/buergertelefon/)** - Berlin.de
