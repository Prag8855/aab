A [VAT](/glossary/Umsatzsteuer) declaration (*Umsatzsteuererklärung* or *UStE*) is a yearly report you send to the *[[Finanzamt]]*. It tells them how much VAT you charged, how much VAT you paid, and how much VAT you owe the *Finanzamt*.
