Title: What is a Mietschuldenfreiheitsbescheinigung?
German_term: Mietschuldenfreiheitsbescheinigung
English_term: Proof that you have no rent debts
Description: 
Date_created: 2018-07-31
Date_updated: 2022-04-20
Related_guides: guides/find-a-flat-in-berlin.md

The *Mietschuldenfreiheitsbescheinigung* is a document you get from your landlord. It confirms that you don't owe any money to your landlord. This document is useful when you apply for an apartment.
