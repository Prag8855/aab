---
Title: What is a Mietschuldenfreiheitsbescheinigung?
German_term: Mietschuldenfreiheitsbescheinigung
English_term: Certificate of freedom from rental debt
Description: This document proves that you don't owe your landlord money.
Date_created: 2018-07-31
Related_guides:
    guides/find-a-flat-in-berlin.md
---

The *Mietschuldenfreiheitsbescheinigung* proves that you have no rental debt. You can get this document from your landlord when you [move out](/guides/moving-out). You might need it to apply for an apartment.
