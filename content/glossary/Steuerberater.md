A tax advisor (*Steuerberater*) helps you with your personal and business taxes. They can file your taxes, do your bookkeeping, [register your business](/guides/fragebogen-zur-steuerlichen-erfassung), handle employee payrolls, and more.

*Steuerberater* is a protected title in Germany. Only people with specific qualifications can call themselves *Steuerberater*. Bookkeeper (*[[Buchhalter]]*) is not a protected title. - [More information](/guides/steuerberater-vs-buchhalter)
