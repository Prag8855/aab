---
Title: What is a Gesundheitskarte?
German_term: Gesundheitskarte
English_term: Health insurance card
Description: A card that confirms your health insurance coverage when you go to the doctor.
Date_created: 2025-05-28
Related_guides:
    guides/german-health-insurance.md
---

When you have [public health insurance](/glossary/gesetzliche%20Krankenversicherung), you get a member card (*Gesundheitskarte*). When you go to the doctor, you show this card at the front desk, and you get healthcare.

!["Example Gesundheitskarte"](/images/elektronische-gesundheitskarte.png "An example Gesundheitskarte.")

This card is also a [European Health Insurance Card](/glossary/EHIC). You can use it to get healthcare in other EU countries.
