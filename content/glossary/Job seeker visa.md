The job seeker visa is a type of [[National Visa]]. It allows you to come to Germany to find work. The job seeker visa can't be extended.[^0] It's valid for 6 to 18 months, depending on your situation.[^1] You must have a university degree or vocational training to apply for it.[^1]

After you find a job, you can [apply for a work visa or a Blue Card](/guides/german-work-visa). If you are self-employed, you can [apply for a freelance visa](/guides/how-to-get-a-german-freelance-visa).

**[Information about the job seeker visa](https://service.berlin.de/dienstleistung/324661/en/)** - Berlin.de
