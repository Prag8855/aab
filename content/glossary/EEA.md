The European Economic Area (EEA) includes all European Union countries, plus Iceland, Liechtenstein and Norway.[^1] Switzerland is not in the EEA.

Citizens of the EEA can live and work in other EEA countries. They do not need a [residence permit](/glossary/Aufenthaltstitel).
