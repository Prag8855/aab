Private health insurance (*private Krankenversicherung* or *PKV*) is a type of health insurance in Germany. The price of private health insurance depends on your age and health condition. Around 11% of Germans have private health insurance. Most people have public health insurance (*[[gesetzliche Krankenversicherung]]* or *[GKV](/glossary/gesetzliche%20Krankenversicherung)*).
