Title: What is a health insurance Zusatzbeitrag?
German_term: Zusatzbeitrag
English_term: Extra insurance contribution

Date_created: 2021-02-01
Date_updated: 2022-04-20

Public health insurers charge an extra contribution (*Zusatzbeitrag*). It allows them to offer extra services. It's between 0% and 2.7% of your income,[^0] but your employer pays half of it. If you pick a health insurer with a lower *Zusatzbeitrag*, you will pay less for health insurance.

[^0]: [krankenkassen.de](https://www.krankenkassen.de/gesetzliche-krankenkassen/krankenkasse-beitrag/kein-zusatzbeitrag/)
