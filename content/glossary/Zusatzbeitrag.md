[Public health insurers](/glossary/Krankenkasse) charge an extra contribution (*Zusatzbeitrag*). It allows them to offer extra services. It's between 0% and 3% of your income,[^0] but your employer pays half of it. If you pick a health insurer with a lower *Zusatzbeitrag*, you pay less for health insurance.
