The *Meldebescheinigung* is a copy of your registration certificate (*[[Anmeldebestätigung]]*). It's a proof of where you live.

Get a *Meldebescheinigung* to...

- Show a more recent proof of where you live
- Replace a lost registration certificate (*Anmeldebestätigung*)
