The tax number (*Steuernummer*) is a unique number that identifies businesses and freelancers. It's a 10 or 11 digit number, and has the format "**12/345/67890**". It's sometimes written in the unified federal format as "30**12**0**34567890**", where the first two digits are the number of your *Bundesland*[^0].

You obtain this number when you submit the *[Fragebogen zur steuerlichen Erfassung](/guides/fragebogen-zur-steuerlichen-erfassung)* to the tax office (*[[Finanzamt]]*). The *Steuernummer* is not the same as a tax ID (*[[Steueridentifikationsnummer]]*) or VAT number (*[[Umsatzsteuernummer]]*).

Your *Steuernummer* is not private. It should appear on your invoices, and in your [[Impressum]]. If you move to a different area, your *Steuernummer* will change.
