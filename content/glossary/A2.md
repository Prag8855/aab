There are 6 levels of German proficiency: A1, A2, B1, B2, C1 and C2.

A2 German is beginner German. It means that you can...[^1]

- Use simple sentences to describe your family, your living conditions, your education and your job.
- Handle very short social exchanges, even if you don't understand enough to keep the conversation going.
- Find specific, predictable information in advertisements, flyers, menus and timetables
- Write a very simple personal letter, for example to thank someone for something.
