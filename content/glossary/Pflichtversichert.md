If you are compulsorily insured (*Pflichtversichert*), you must choose [public health insurance](/glossary/gesetzliche%20Krankenversicherung). You can't have [private health insurance](/glossary/private%20Krankenversicherung). It also means that [public health insurers](/glossary/Krankenkasse) must accept you, even if you are very sick.

If you are an employee, and you make under €{{GKV_FREIWILLIG_VERSICHERT_MIN_INCOME|cur}} per year, you are *pflichtversichert*.
