The citizens' office (*Bürgeramt*) handles many aspects of German bureaucracy. This is where you [register your address](/guides/anmeldung-in-english-berlin), [exchange a foreign driving licence](/guides/canadian-german-driving-license), [apply for an International Driving Permit](/guides/international-driving-permit-germany) and more. If you need a piece of paper, you often get it from the *Bürgeramt*.

**Useful link:** [List of *Bürgeramt* locations in Berlin](https://service.berlin.de/standorte/buergeraemter/)
