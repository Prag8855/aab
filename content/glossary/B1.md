There are 6 levels of German proficiency: A1, A2, B1, B2, C1 and C2.

B1 German is intermediate German. It means that you can...[^1]

- Deal with everyday situations at work, school and at home.
- Talk about familiar topics without preparation.
- Understand text and speech in clear language about familiar topics.
- Write simple texts about familiar topics.
