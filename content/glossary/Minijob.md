In Germany, a minijob is a job that pays {{MINIJOB_MAX_INCOME|cur}}€/month or less.[^0]

Minijobs have a few benefits:

- If you only have a minijob, you pay no [income tax](/glossary/Einkommensteuer),[^1] [unemployment insurance](/glossary/Arbeitslosenversicherung) or [public pension contributions](/glossary/gesetzliche%20Rentenversicherung).
- If you only have a minijob, you can use your spouse's or your parents' [public health insurance](/glossary/gesetzliche%20Krankenversicherung) for free.[^2]
