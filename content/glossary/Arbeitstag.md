In Germany, business days (*Arbeitstage*) usually mean Monday to Friday, excluding official holidays.[^0] This is different from work days (*[Werktage](/glossary/Werktag)*), which are usually Monday to Saturday, excluding official holidays.

[^0]: [de.wikipedia.org](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arbeitstag#Rechtsfragen)
