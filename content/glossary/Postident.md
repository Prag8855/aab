*Postident* is a service that lets you verify your identity at the post office. You print a document, go to the post office and show your passport. Then the post office tells the company that your identity is verified.

*Postident* is often used when you [open a bank account](/guides/first-bank-account-in-germany), if they can't verify your documents with a video call.
