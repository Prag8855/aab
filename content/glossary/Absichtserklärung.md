A letter of intent (*[[Absichtserklärung]]*) is a letter that says "I plan to hire this person to do this service at this price". You need these letters to [apply for a freelance visa](/guides/how-to-get-a-german-freelance-visa) in Berlin.

A letter of intent is not a contract. The person who wrote the letter is not forced to do anything.
