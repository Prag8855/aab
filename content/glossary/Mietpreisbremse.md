The rent price brake (*Mietpreisbremse*) is a rent control law in Berlin. It limits how much rent the landlord can ask for.

The *Mietpreisbremse* is not the same as the *[[Mietendeckel]]*. The *Mietendeckel* was repealed in April 2021.
