Title: What is BAföG
German_term: BAföG
English_term: Financial support for students
Description: A type of financial aid for German students.
Date_created: 2018-07-31
Related_guides: guides/gez-rundfunkbeitrag.md, guides/cost-of-living.md

*BAföG* (*Bundesausbildungsförderungsgesetz*) is financial support for students. A *BAföG* recipient is a student who gets financial support from the state.
