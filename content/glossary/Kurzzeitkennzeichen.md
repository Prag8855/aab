Title: What are Kurzzeitkennzeichen?
German_term: Kurzzeitkennzeichen
English_term: Temporary licence plate
Description: 
Date_created: 2019-01-17
Related_guides: guides/buying-used-car-in-germany.md

Temporary licence plates (*Kurzzeitkennzeichen*) allows you to drive a vehicle for a short time. For example, it can be used to test a vehicle that has no licence plate.
