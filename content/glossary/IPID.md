All [EU](/glossary/European%20Union) insurance policies come with an Insurance Product Information Document (IPID).[^0] This is a one-page summary of what your insurance covers.

When you [choose health insurance](/guides/german-health-insurance) or [liability insurance](/guides/haftpflichtversicherung), you should always read the IPID.

[^0]: [cignaglobal.com](https://www.cignaglobal.com/individuals-families/members/resources/insurance-product-information-documents)
