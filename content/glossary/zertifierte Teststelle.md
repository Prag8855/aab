In Berlin, a certified testing centre (*zertifierte Teststelle*) is any place where you can get an official PCR or rapid antigen test. This includes the state-owned test centres, and the hundreds of businesses that test people.

**[List of certified test locations](https://www.direkttesten.berlin/)** - Berlin.de
