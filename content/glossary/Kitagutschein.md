A *Kitagutschein* is a voucher that allows you to get free childcare in Berlin. You need this document to send your child to a *[[Kita]]*. The *Kitagutschein* says how many hours of *Kita* your child can have. Many Kitas require a *Kitagutschein* to put you on their waiting list[^0]*.*
