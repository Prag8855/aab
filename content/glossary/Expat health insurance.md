Expat health insurance or incoming health insurance is a type of temporary health insurance. You use this insurance to get your German visa and move to Germany. When you arrive to Germany, you should switch to [public](/glossary/gesetzliche%20Krankenversicherung) or [private health insurance](/glossary/private%20Krankenversicherung).
