A self-employed (*Selbst채ndig*) person is someone who runs their own business.

In some cases, like in immigration law, *Selbst채ndiger* means the same thing as *[[Gewerbetreibender]]*: a self-employed person who is not a freelancer (*[[Freiberufler]]*).
