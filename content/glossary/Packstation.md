Title: What is a Packstation?
German_term: Packstation
English_term: Package pickup station

Date_created: 2019-02-10
Related_guides: guides/package-delivery.md

A *Packstation* is a place where you can receive packages, instead of having them delivered to your house. *Packstations* are sometimes more reliable than receiving packages at home. It works like a *[[Paketshop]]*, but fully automated.

![A DHL Packstation in Berlin](/images/DHL-Packstation.jpg "A DHL Packstation")
