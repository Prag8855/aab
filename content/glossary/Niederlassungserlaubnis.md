German permanent residence (*PR* or *Niederlassungserlaubnis*) lets you live, work and study in Germany. You can get permanent residence after 21 to 60 months in Germany.

It's better than a [residence permit](/glossary/Aufenthaltstitel) because...

- It's permanent. You don't need to renew it.
- It's flexible. It's not tied to your income or occupation.
- It's more trusted. It's easier to get credit or a mortgage.

It's not as good as German citizenship because...[^1]

- You can't vote in elections.
- If you leave Germany for a long time, you lose it.
- If you commit a serious crime, you can lose it.

[^1]: [migrando.de](https://migrando.de/niederlassungserlaubnis/)
