A National Visa (also known as Type D or [Category D Visa](/glossary/Category%20D%20visa)) is a visa for long-term stays in Germany[^0]. It allows you to move to Germany. When you arrive to Germany, you can convert your National Visa to a [residence permit](/glossary/Aufenthaltstitel) at the *[[Ausländerbehörde]]*.

[^0]: [germany.info](https://www.germany.info/us-en/service/visa/residence-visa/922288)
