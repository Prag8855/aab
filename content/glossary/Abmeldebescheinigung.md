The deregistration certificate (*Abmeldebescheinigung*) confirms that you have [deregistered your address](/guides/abmeldung-deregister-in-berlin) in Germany. You get this document when you do your *[[Abmeldung]]*. If you do your *Abmeldung* by mail or by email, the *Abmeldebescheinigung* is sent to your new address.

If you already left Germany, you can email any Bürgeramt and ask for an *Abmeldebescheinigung*. They will send it to you by mail. This costs 10€.

This is not the same as the [*A<u>n</u>meldebescheinigung*](/glossary/Meldebescheinigung). You get an *Anmeldebescheinigung* when you [register your address](/glossary/Anmeldung).
