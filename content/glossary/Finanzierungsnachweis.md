A proof of financial resources (*Finanzierungsnachweis*) is required when applying for foreign student, au-pair, language student and job-seeker visas. Usually, this means you have to open a blocking account (*[[Sperrkonto]]*). [Fintiba](/out/fintiba) and [X-patrio](https://www.x-patrio.com/blocked-account/exploration) offer blocking accounts.
