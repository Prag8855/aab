---
Title: What is a Volkshochschule?
German_term: Volkshochschule
English_term: Community college
Description: A community college that offers all sorts of classes for adults.
Date_created: 2021-04-01
Related_guides: guides/learn-german.md
---

A *Volkshochschule* or *VHS* is the German equivalent of a community college. They offer different courses for adults, including language classes.
