Title: Who is the Wohnungsgeber?
German_term: Wohnungsgeber
English_term: Landlord or apartment-giver

Date_created: 2018-07-31
Related_guides: guides/anmeldung-in-english-berlin.md

The *Wohnungsgeber* (the "apartment-giver") is the person who gives you a room or an apartment to rent. It can be the landlord, or the main tenant (*[[Hauptmieter]]*).[^0] The main tenant must get the permission of the landlord to sublet a room or the entire apartment. [In some situations](/guides/anmeldung-in-english-berlin#can-the-landlord-refuse-to-let-me-register), the landlord can refuse.[^1]
