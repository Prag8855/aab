Title: What is a Deckungszusage?
German_term: Deckungszusage
English_term: Insurance cover note
Description: 
Date_created: 2021-09-18
Date_updated: 2022-04-20
Related_guides: guides/legal-insurance.md

A cover note (*Deckungszusage*) is a confirmation from your insurance company. It confirms that your [legal insurance](/glossary/Rechtsschutzversicherung) will pay for your lawyer and legal costs.
