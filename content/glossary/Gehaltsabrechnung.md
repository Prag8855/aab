A pay slip (*Gehaltsabrechnung*) shows how much you earned, and what contributions you have paid. You get it from your employer every month. It looks like [this](/images/Gehaltsabrechnung-muster-example.png).

You can use your *Gehaltsabrechnung* to find your tax ID (*[[Steueridentifikationsnummer]]*) and your social insurance number (*[[Sozialversicherungsnummer]]*).
