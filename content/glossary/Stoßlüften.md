---
Title: What is Stoßlüften?
German_term: Stoßlüften
English_term: Ventilation
Description: 
Date_created: 2022-10-04
Related_guides: guides/moving-in.md
---

Germans ventilate their apartments every day, even in winter. They open all the windows for a few minutes to let fresh air in. It's called *Stoßlüften*.

Ventilation is so important that it's sometimes required in your lease.
