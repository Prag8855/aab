Members of certain churches must pay the church tax (*Kirchensteuer*). It's **9% of your [income tax](/glossary/Einkommensteuer)**, or 8% in Bavaria and Baden-Württemberg.[^2]

If you earn 50,000€ per year, the church tax is around 800€ per year. Use my [tax calculator](/tools/tax-calculator) to calculate your church tax.

These religious communities collect a church tax:[^3]

- Evangelical Church (*Evangelische Kirche*)
- Roman Catholic Church (*Römisch-katholische Kirche*)
- Jewish Community (*Jüdische Gemeinde*)
- Old Catholic Church (*Altkatholische Kirche*)
- Some Free Religious communities (*Freireligiöse Gemeinden*)

If you are not part of those churches, you do not pay church tax.[^1]

You declare your religion when you [register your address](/guides/anmeldung-in-english-berlin). The church can also tax you if you were a member in another country.[^4] To [stop paying the church tax](https://www.settle-in-berlin.com/stop-paying-german-church-tax/), you must leave the church.
