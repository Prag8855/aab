Title: What is an Arbeitszeugnis
German_term: Arbeitszeugnis
English_term: Job reference letter
Description: A letter you get from your employer that confirms that you worked there.
Date_created: 2023-03-28
Related_guides: guides/losing-your-job.md
---

A job reference letter (*Arbeitszeugnis*) is a letter you get from your employer. It proves that you worked at that company.

This letter usually explains when you worked for the employer, and what type of work you did.

When you leave a job, you have the right to get an *Arbeitszeugnis*.[^1]
