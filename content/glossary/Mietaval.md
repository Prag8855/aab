Date_created: 2021-06-20

If you don't have enough money for your [apartment deposit](/glossary/Kaution), you can get a *Mietaval*. The bank pays your deposit, and you pay a fee to the bank.[^0]

[^0]: [dkb.de](https://bank.dkb.de/fragen-antworten)
