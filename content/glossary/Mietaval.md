If you don't have enough money for your apartment deposit, you can get a *Mietaval*. The bank pays your deposit, and you pay a fee to the bank[^0].

[^0]: [dkb.de](https://www.dkb.de/kundenservice/haeufige_fragen/mietaval.html)
