If you have a [residence permit](/glossary/Aufenthaltstitel), and you [quit or lose your job](/guides/losing-your-job), you must tell the *[[Ausländerbehörde]]*. Use this template to write to them.

**[What happens to your residence permit ➞](/guides/losing-your-job#what-happens-to-your-residence-permit)**

If you have [permanent residence](/glossary/Niederlassungserlaubnis), EU or [[EEA]] citizenship, you don't need to do this.

## How to send this letter

In Berlin, you can email, post or fax this letter. It's better if you send it by fax or [registered mail](/glossary/Einschreibebrief).

Don't forget to include a copy of your resignation letter, your termination notice, and your new work contract.
