When you [apply for the German freelance visa](/guides/how-to-get-a-german-freelance-visa), you must include [letters of intent](/glossary/Absichtserkl%C3%A4rung) from potential clients. It shows that you will find clients in Germany.

This document explains what a letter of intent should contain. See the [letter of intent example](/docs/letter-of-intent-example-german-freelance-visa) for the final result. To translate your letters of intent, use [Lingoking](/out/lingoking).
