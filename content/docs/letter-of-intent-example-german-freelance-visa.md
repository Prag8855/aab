This [letter of intent](/glossary/Absichtserkl%C3%A4rung) was used by a software developer to [apply for a freelance visa](/guides/how-to-get-a-german-freelance-visa) in Berlin. The personal information was changed for privacy reasons.

**Do not copy this letter word for word**. Your potential clients must write their own letters of intent. [These instructions](/docs/german-freelance-visa-letter-of-intent) can help them. To translate your letters of intent to German, use [Lingoking](/out/lingoking).
