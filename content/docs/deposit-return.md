After you [move out](/guides/moving-out) of an apartment, you can get your *[[Kaution]]* back. Some landlords take a really long time to return your *Kaution*. Use this letter generator to ask for your *Kaution* back.

## How to send this letter

This letter must be **printed and signed**. Send it by [registered mail](/glossary/Einschreibebrief) to make sure that it's delivered. An email, a fax, an instant message or a conversation is not enough.

**[How to send a registered letter ➞](/guides/mail-a-letter#by-registered-mail)**
